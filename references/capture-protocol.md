# Capture Protocol

> How the skills capture material as the owner works. Shared by `dm-sales-coach`, `respond-to-own`,
> `log-prospect`, `commenting-others`, and `post`. The rule: **offer, never save silently; draft until
> the owner confirms it's true and theirs.** Four buckets, handled differently.

## The four buckets

1. **Owner's own story / lived scene** → append to the **Story bank** in `references/about-me.md`
   as *scene → the point it proves → which lens it fits*. Offer: "add this to your Story bank?" Confirm
   before it stays. Never fabricate or embellish.

2. **Owner's belief / contrarian take** → append under the matching lens in the **Beliefs & contrarian
   takes** list in `references/positioning.md`. Trigger: a sharp, distinctly-theirs belief or contrarian
   take they just articulated (the kind that satisfies rule 1(b) in the reply skills). Offer to save; tag
   it with the lens. (Foundational beliefs live in `about-me.md` BELIEFS; this list holds the
   content-ready, spiky ones.)

3. **Testimonial (someone praising the owner or their work)** → `references/testimonials.md`, captured
   verbatim with who / where / date and `Consent to publish = not granted` by default. **Never publish
   without consent.** Financial testimonials need prior WRITTEN consent, no PII, no figures (Mode 2, see
   gates.md); coaching testimonials still need consent before use. Offer to capture and flag that consent
   is required before it can be used anywhere.

4. **Someone else's personal story / situation** → **do NOT capture as reusable content.** This is the
   owner's hard rule: never spend other people's stories. The most allowed is a private note on their
   row in `trackers/lead-gen-tracker.xlsx` for relationship continuity. Never something `post` can pull
   from, never published.

## Always
- Surface it inline as an *offer* ("want me to save this?"), never auto-write.
- Draft only from what was actually said; confirm it's true and theirs before it stays.
- Keep compliance: financial material stays concept-only (no numbers, products, returns).
- A captured-but-unconfirmed item is a draft, not a saved entry.
